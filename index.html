A Sikh Boy Who Found Islam

Yazdan Shaikh — formerly known as Param — was born into a Sikh family. After years of honest introspection, searching, and sincere reflection, he embarked on one of the most profound journeys a soul can take.

During the blessed month of Ramadan 2023, he embraced Islam and adopted the name Yazdan — meaning "Divine Grace" — as a testament to where his heart had found its eternal home.

Today, with over 926K YouTube subscribers, he stands as a powerful voice of Quran, Sunnah, Akhlaq, and Tawakkul — while also empowering thousands through Yazdan Academy, his digital learning platform.
